English Posts from Today On

I hadbeen considering using English, a more wide-accepted language, to write down and publish my new posts.

Such ideas had been stronger and stronger after I returned back from University of Oxford, and University of Cambridge, where I met with lots of new friends. They said that they were, and are, really interested in me, and my major. It is indeed a pity, though, that it happened to be a personal website written in Chinese: but not anymore.

Moreover, during the days in UK, I became worried about my writing abilities. Although I have been quite confident in communicating with people fluently, my writing ability remained relatively weak. Then I came into a cool idea, that I will reorganize this lovely website, and adjust them in the favor of more friends from English-spoken countries or not, without the problems and barriers of languages.

This is also the initial reason why I set up this website. From today on, I am going to change this website into English, and from now on, most of the posts shall be written in English.
