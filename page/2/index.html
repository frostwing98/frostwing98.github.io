Willkommen!

I am frostwing98, a.k.a. Allen Yuqing Yang. Welcome to my personal blog!

I am currently in my third year as a junior software engineering student in Software Institute, Nanjing University. I am expected to obtain a bachelor's degree in engineering in 2020, and am currently actively looking for openning PhD positions in US universities.

My research interests lie in software analysis, software verification and information security, more specifically, I am particularly interested in mobile software security, third-library security, user privacy leaking and how to combine information retrieval techniques to solve security issues and make systems more dependable. If you are also interested in my research field(s), please feel free to check my CV.

More detailed informations about my research, academic services &experiences and personal interests can be found here.

I wrote several articles about my daily life, personal experiences in college, and my projects. I am planning to finish a tiny book about my four years in Nanjing University. Please feel free to check the Archives or use the Tag or Categories to filter my posts.
