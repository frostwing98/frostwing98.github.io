Willkommen!

I am frostwing98, a.k.a. Allen Yuqing Yang. Welcome to my personal blog!

My research interests lie in program analysis, IoT and mobile security, and user privacy leakage. More specifically, I am interested in performing security-oriented measurement studies for emerging platforms and automatically detecting security vulnerabilities and software defects with program analysis and documentation analysis. If you are also interested in my research field(s), please feel free to check my CV.

I will persue a Ph.D. degree in Ohio State University in Mobile Security, advised by Dr Zhiqiang Lin.

Feel free to explore in my Archives. For more information about my awards, honors, academic services, please visit About page.
