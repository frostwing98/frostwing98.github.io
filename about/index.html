Willkommen!

I am a PhD student in Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering in Ohio State University. I am interested in IoT and mobile security, and user privacy leakage. More specifically, I am particularly interested in building systems with HIGH RELIABILITY AND LOW LATENCY by adoping techniques from security, software, architecture, and other fields in Computer Science.
