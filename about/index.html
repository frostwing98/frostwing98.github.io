Who am I

Willkommen!

I am frostwing98, enrolled in Nanjing University in 2016, majoring in Software Engineering. I am interested in Network Attack and Defence, as well as the CTF contests. Currently, I am engaged in undergraduate research training under supervision of Prof. Fengyuan Xu and Asst. Prof. Hongyu Kuang. See my CV for more details.

Research, Projects and Services

Research

Currently I am collaborating with two brilliant teachers and advancing in two interesting fields: Prof. Fengyuan Xu in COSEC, and Assistant Prof. Hongyu Kuang, in Software Institute.

  • I am interested in two different fields: One is security related, i.e. software analysis & verification, system security and mobile/IoT security, and the other is IR(information retrieval,see here) related, i.e. IR in software tracebility and IR in system protection. More specifically, I am particularly interested in mobile software security, third-library security, user privacy leaking and how to combine information retrieval techniques to solve security issues and make systems more dependable:
    • I have started a new project with Assoc Prof. Jingyu Hua. It is about how to use side channel information to localize certain hidden digital device that may be secretly filming in public hotels.
    • In the research of learning model security, I am co-operating with Prof Xu, and Hao Wu, who is currently pursuing his phd. degree in CS department.
    • In the software information retrieval research, I am cooperating with Assistant Prof. Kuang, leading a tiny group with two M.Eng. students who are pursuing their M.Eng. degree in 2021.
    • I also completed a project with supervision of Assoc. Prof. Jingyu Hua: the MAIL indoor AP localization project. The project achieved great accuracy.
    • Moreover, I also led a 9-person group under a comprehensive empirical software engineering literature review project. For full slides and reports, please visit here.
  • Besides, I used to be a member of the Nanjing University Trinity CTF team.
