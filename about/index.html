Who am I

Willkommen!

I am frostwing98, enrolled in Nanjing University in 2016, majoring in Software Engineering. I am interested in Network Attack and Defence, as well as the CTF contests. Currently, I am engaged in undergraduate research training under supervision of Prof. Fengyuan Xu and Asst. Prof. Hongyu Kuang. See my CV for more details.

Research, Projects and Services

Research

Currently I am collaborating with two brilliant teachers and advancing in two interesting fields: Prof. Fengyuan Xu in COSEC, and Assistant Prof. Hongyu Kuang, in Software Institute.

  • I am interested in two different fields: Cybersecurity, especially in verification of learning models and privacy preserving, and, Information Retrieval, especially from Information generated during Software Development:
    • In the research of learning model security, I am co-operating with Prof Xu, and Hao Wu, who is currently pursuing his phd. degree in CS department.
    • In the software information retrieval research, I am cooperating with Assistant Prof. Kuang, leading a tiny group with two students who is expected to graduate this summer for their graduation thesis.
    • I also completed a project with supervision of Assoc. Prof. Jingyu Hua: the MAIL indoor AP localization project. The project achieved great accuracy.
    • Moreover, I am also leading a 9-person group under a comprehensive empirical software engineering literature review project.
  • Besides, I am a member of the Nanjing University Trinity CTF team previously.
  • Please note: further discussions and details of my research interest ,status and plans will be included in my research proposal, which is expected to be available before July.
